HEALTHY RECIPES

Ragi Laddu

A healthy, nutrient-packed sweet made from roasted ragi flour, jaggery, ghee, and nuts. Rich in calcium, iron, and fiber, these laddus support bone health, digestion, and provide a natural energy boost. Perfect as a nutritious snack

Palak Paratha

A healthy Indian flatbread made with spinach and whole wheat flour. Packed with iron and fiber, it makes for a nutritious breakfast or meal option, great for boosting immunity and digestion.

Kerala Avial

A traditional Kerala dish made with mixed vegetables, coconut, and yogurt, cooked in a flavorful blend of curry leaves and coconut oil. Rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, Avial is a healthy and delicious side dish.

Banana Almond Butter Bites

Frozen banana slices topped with almond butter, chocolate chips, and a sprinkle of sea salt, creating a satisfying and healthy snack.

Mango & Avocado Smoothie

A creamy and tropical smoothie made with ripe mango, avocado, coconut water, and a touch of lime for a refreshing drink..

Fruit Salad with Yogurt and Honey

This fruit salad is a nutrient-packed snack or breakfast option. The combination of fresh fruits provides essential vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber to support your immune system and digestive health.
